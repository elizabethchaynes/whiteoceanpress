About the Press

Small press,
large impact

White Ocean Press is an independent publisher focusing on inspirational books of literature, memoir, self-help, poetry, and more. All books are professionally edited and properly formatted.


It is based in the New York City area and managed by Elizabeth C. Haynes. Learn more about her at elizabethchaynes.com.

Submissions

We are not accepting yet

We are not currently seeking submissions. Check back in the future for updates! When we do open, we will accept the following:

  • Literary fiction (novels and novellas)
  • Collections of essays
  • Poetry collections (full-length only)
  • No genre fiction, thrillers, or YA at this time
  • Previously unpublished work only
